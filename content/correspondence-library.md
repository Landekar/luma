Each genre world page on Coalesce draws from the relevant sections of this library.

## GENRE WORLD DERIVATIONS
### How each genre world maps to this library

### GOTHIC DARK FANTASY
**Sonic palette sources:** Pipe organ, full orchestra (tutti), cello section, bass-baritone, Gregorian chant, cathedral reverb, timpani, double bass
**Key character:** Dense harmonic mass, sacred weight, ancient resonance, long decay
**Visual lock:** Stone, darkness, vertical scale, ornate detail, warm candlelight against cold void

### RETRO-FUTURISM (UTOPIA)
**Sonic palette sources:** Theremin, polysynth pads (Juno/JP-8), big band brass, orchestral swells, vocoder, tape saturation, vibraphone
**Key character:** Optimistic synthetic warmth, Space Age brightness, analog future that never arrived
**Visual lock:** Chrome, turquoise, coral, cream — Googie architecture, Sid Mead, Soviet cosmic poster art

### ANALOGISM
**Sonic palette sources:** Minimoog/monosynth, Rhodes piano, tape saturation, heavy compression, modular/generative, FM synthesis (DX7), room reverb (small)
**Key character:** Warm machine imperfection, analog grain, deliberate limitation as aesthetic
**Visual lock:** Amber phosphor screens, brushed aluminum, worn Bakelite, VU meters, dark hardware

### CYBERPUNK
**Sonic palette sources:** Industrial/mechanical texture, 808 bass, polysynth pads (dark), glitch/digital artifact, vocoder, deep delay, wavetable synthesis, arpeggiator sequences
**Key character:** High density, neon-lit darkness, synthetic humanity, urban pressure
**Visual lock:** Deep violet + neon cyan/pink, wet concrete, holographic film, vertical signage overload

### PASTORAL FOLK
**Sonic palette sources:** Solo flute, acoustic guitar (implied), hurdy-gurdy, pan flute, hand percussion, field recordings/natural ambience, fire crackle, small room reverb
**Key character:** Organic imperfection, breath and wood, seasonal melancholy, earth-connected
**Visual lock:** Mossy green, warm ochre, rough linen, aged wood, watercolor soft focus, dappled light
